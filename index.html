ABOUT WEISHIN

維新鋁業股份有限公司 WEISHIN Industry Co., Ltd.

維新鋁業股份有限公司創立於1972年，是台灣最早期的民營鋁擠型工廠之一，位於717台南市仁德區二行里二仁路一段491巷31號。 半個世紀以來，公司秉持「維新品質、永續經營」的理念，以職人精神持續精進製造技術，創造時代尖端製品。

WEISHIN Industry Co., Ltd. was founded in 1972 as one of Taiwan's earliest private aluminum extrusion manufacturers. Located in Rende District, Tainan, the company has upheld its philosophy of "WEISHIN Quality, Sustainable Management" for over 50 years, continuously refining manufacturing technology with craftsmanship spirit.

公司導入ISO 9001:2015品質管理系統，從原料選材、模具開發、擠型生產到出貨檢驗全程數位化管理，並在專業人才培訓與高精密儀器設備更新上持續投入，穩健的製程能力走在同業先端。

The company has implemented ISO 9001:2015 quality management with full digitalization from raw material sourcing, mold development, extrusion production to shipment inspection. Continuous investment in professional talent training and high-precision equipment keeps WEISHIN at the forefront of the industry.

維新歷史
OUR HISTORY

半世紀的技術傳承 50 Years of Technical Heritage

第一章 — 維新的原點 1972–1981
Chapter 1 — The Origin 1972–1981
1972
創辦人吳真投資新台幣1,500萬元，在彰化花壇創立維新鋁業，為台灣最早期民營鋁擠型工廠之一。初期員工35人，年產能2,500噸。
Founder Wu Chen invested NT$15M to establish WEISHIN in Changhua. One of Taiwan's earliest private aluminum extrusion factories. 35 employees, 2,500-ton annual capacity.
1973
遷廠至台南現址。投資額增至4,500萬元，廠區面積5,000坪，員工達100人，年產能3,000噸。
Relocated to current Tainan facility. Investment increased to NT$45M. Factory area: 5,000 ping. Employees: 100. Annual capacity: 3,000 tons.
1979
增設1,700噸擠型機，引進日本安川電機擠型引出機，正式進入自動化生產時代。
Added 1,700-ton extrusion press. Introduced Japan Yaskawa Electric puller — formal entry into automation era.
1980
進入軍品市場，通過國防部聯勤評鑑，取得「國防工業合格衛星工廠」認證。
Entered military products market. Passed Ministry of Defense evaluation. Obtained "Defense Product Industry Qualified Satellite Factory" certification.
第二章 — 維新起飛 1982–1996
Chapter 2 — Taking Off 1982–1996
1982
購置廠東側土地，建設辦公大樓與廠房。新增1,700噸及1,000噸擠型機各兩套，年產能提升至12,000噸。
Built new office building and factories. Added 1,700-ton and 1,000-ton extrusion machines. Annual capacity increased to 12,000 tons.
1987
引進亞洲第一套Wagstaff Airslip鋁合金鑄造系統，品質與鑄造技術達到世界一流水準。
Introduced Asia's first Wagstaff Airslip aluminum alloy casting system. Quality and casting technology aligned with world-class manufacturers.
1991
取得經濟部中央標準局甲等品質管理認證，榮獲五項CNS優良標誌。
Obtained Grade A quality management certification. Received five CNS marks of excellence from the Ministry of Economic Affairs.
第三章 — ISO認證時代 1997–2007
Chapter 3 — ISO Certification Era 1997–2007
1997
通過美國UL-9002及中華民國CNS-9002認證。新增2,500噸擠型生產線，年產能突破15,000噸。
Passed US UL-9002 and ROC CNS-9002 certification. Added 2,500-ton extrusion line. Annual capacity exceeded 15,000 tons.
2000
模具課引進線切割機、數位放電加工機、CNC綜合加工機及三次元量測儀與CAD/CAM系統，正式進入數位化時代。
Die shop introduced wire cutting, CNC machining, 3D measurement instruments and CAD/CAM systems — entering the digital era.
2002
ISO-9002升版為ISO-9001品質管理系統，全面導入ERP系統，所有製程全面數位化。
Converted ISO-9002 to ISO-9001. Implemented company-wide ERP system — all processes fully digitized.
2006
新增間接擠型機生產線，供應航太及軍工高科技廠商高合金鋁擠型材。
Added indirect extrusion line. Supplies aerospace and military high-tech manufacturers with high-alloy aluminum extrusion materials.
第四章 — 全程數位化生產 2008–今
Chapter 4 — Full Digitalization 2008–Present
2008
再增第二條2,500噸擠型生產線，年產能達18,000噸。
Added second 2,500-ton extrusion line. Annual capacity reached 18,000 tons.
2014
更換老舊1,100噸及1,800噸設備，年產能提升至21,000噸。
Replaced aging equipment. Annual production capacity increased to 21,000 tons.
2019
新增1,800噸一體化間接擠型機，供應工業高合金材料，擴大產能滿足客戶需求。
Added integrated 1,800-ton indirect extrusion machine for industrial high-alloy materials.
2020
更新製程設備，推進先進數位自動化流程，提升效率與品質，達成公司品質政策目標。
Advanced digital automated processes implemented. Maximized quality and capacity. Achieved company quality policy goals.

專精高品質、高精密鋁擠型生產，配備頂尖擠型設備，先進數位化生產管理，持續優化鋁合金擠型製造製程。

Specializes in high-quality, high-precision aluminum extrusion production with top-tier equipment and advanced digital production management.

累積50年專業經驗，先進設備投入，嚴格製程管控，確保材料潔淨度與品質穩定性，專供航太及軍工高規格需求。
