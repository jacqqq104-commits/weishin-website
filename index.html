ABOUT WEISHIN

維新鋁業股份有限公司 WEISHIN Industry Co., Ltd.

維新鋁業股份有限公司創立於1972年，是台灣最早期的民營鋁擠型工廠之一，位於717台南市仁德區二行里二仁路一段491巷31號。 半個世紀以來，公司秉持「維新品質、永續經營」的理念，以職人精神持續精進製造技術，創造時代尖端製品。

WEISHIN Industry Co., Ltd. was founded in 1972 as one of Taiwan's earliest private aluminum extrusion manufacturers. Located in Rende District, Tainan, the company has upheld its philosophy of "WEISHIN Quality, Sustainable Management" for over 50 years, continuously refining manufacturing technology with craftsmanship spirit.

公司導入ISO 9001:2015品質管理系統，從原料選材、模具開發、擠型生產到出貨檢驗全程數位化管理，並在專業人才培訓與高精密儀器設備更新上持續投入，穩健的製程能力走在同業先端。

The company has implemented ISO 9001:2015 quality management with full digitalization from raw material sourcing, mold development, extrusion production to shipment inspection. Continuous investment in professional talent training and high-precision equipment keeps WEISHIN at the forefront of the industry.

維新歷史
OUR HISTORY

半世紀的技術傳承 50 Years of Technical Heritage

第一章 — 維新的原點 1972–1981
Chapter 1 — The Origin 1972–1981
1972
創辦人吳真投資新台幣1,500萬元，在彰化花壇創立維新鋁業，為台灣最早期民營鋁擠型工廠之一。初期員工35人，年產能2,500噸。
Founder Wu Chen invested NT$15M to establish WEISHIN in Changhua. One of Taiwan's earliest private aluminum extrusion factories. 35 employees, 2,500-ton annual capacity.
1973
遷廠至台南現址。投資額增至4,500萬元，廠區面積5,000坪，員工達100人，年產能3,000噸。
Relocated to current Tainan facility. Investment increased to NT$45M. Factory area: 5,000 ping. Employees: 100. Annual capacity: 3,000 tons.
1979
增設1,700噸擠型機，引進日本安川電機擠型引出機，正式進入自動化生產時代。
Added 1,700-ton extrusion press. Introduced Japan Yaskawa Electric puller — formal entry into automation era.
1980
進入軍品市場，通過國防部聯勤評鑑，取得「國防工業合格衛星工廠」認證。
Entered military products market. Passed Ministry of Defense evaluation. Obtained "Defense Product Industry Qualified Satellite Factory" certification.
第二章 — 維新起飛 1982–1996
Chapter 2 — Taking Off 1982–1996
1982
購置廠東側土地，建設辦公大樓與廠房。新增1,700噸及1,000噸擠型機各兩套，年產能提升至12,000噸。
Built new office building and factories. Added 1,700-ton and 1,000-ton extrusion machines. Annual capacity increased to 12,000 tons.
1987
引進亞洲第一套Wagstaff Airslip鋁合金鑄造系統，品質與鑄造技術達到世界一流水準。
Introduced Asia's first Wagstaff Airslip aluminum alloy casting system. Quality and casting technology aligned with world-class manufacturers.
1991
取得經濟部中央標準局甲等品質管理認證，榮獲五項CNS優良標誌。
Obtained Grade A quality management certification. Received five CNS marks of excellence from the Ministry of Economic Affairs.
第三章 — ISO認證時代 1997–2007
Chapter 3 — ISO Certification Era 1997–2007
1997
通過美國UL-9002及中華民國CNS-9002認證。新增2,500噸擠型生產線，年產能突破15,000噸。
Passed US UL-9002 and ROC CNS-9002 certification. Added 2,500-ton extrusion line. Annual capacity exceeded 15,000 tons.
2000
模具課引進線切割機、數位放電加工機、CNC綜合加工機及三次元量測儀與CAD/CAM系統，正式進入數位化時代。
Die shop introduced wire cutting, CNC machining, 3D measurement instruments and CAD/CAM systems — entering the digital era.
2002
ISO-9002升版為ISO-9001品質管理系統，全面導入ERP系統，所有製程全面數位化。
Converted ISO-9002 to ISO-9001. Implemented company-wide ERP system — all processes fully digitized.
2006
新增間接擠型機生產線，供應航太及軍工高科技廠商高合金鋁擠型材。
Added indirect extrusion line. Supplies aerospace and military high-tech manufacturers with high-alloy aluminum extrusion materials.
第四章 — 全程數位化生產 2008–今
Chapter 4 — Full Digitalization 2008–Present
2008
再增第二條2,500噸擠型生產線，年產能達18,000噸。
Added second 2,500-ton extrusion line. Annual capacity reached 18,000 tons.
2014
更換老舊1,100噸及1,800噸設備，年產能提升至21,000噸。
Replaced aging equipment. Annual production capacity increased to 21,000 tons.
2019
新增1,800噸一體化間接擠型機，供應工業高合金材料，擴大產能滿足客戶需求。
Added integrated 1,800-ton indirect extrusion machine for industrial high-alloy materials.
2020
更新製程設備，推進先進數位自動化流程，提升效率與品質，達成公司品質政策目標。
Advanced digital automated processes implemented. Maximized quality and capacity. Achieved company quality policy goals.

01
Raw Material Management

物料管理課

Raw Material Management

廠內設有原料管理儲存區，國內外進口鋁錠皆經嚴格檢測把關，提供高品質且安全的庫存備料，供加工鋸切與擠型使用。

WEISHIN maintains a raw material storage area for imported billets and domestic round bars. After strict quality inspection, safe stock is prepared for sawing, machining, and extrusion.

  • 國外鋁錠以杜拜 DUBAI 與巴林 ALBA 為主要供應來源，提供 9 吋與 7 吋料徑鋁錠。Foreign billet supply includes DUBAI and ALBA sources for 9-inch and 7-inch alloy billets.
  • 國內鋁錠涵蓋 6061、6063、6082，以及 2014、2024、7050、7075 等高合金材料。Domestic billet supply covers 6061, 6063, 6082, plus high-strength alloys such as 2014, 2024, 7050, and 7075.
  • 出貨產品依客戶需求包裝，提供表面防護並提高運輸穩定性。Finished goods are packed according to customer needs with surface protection for safer transportation.
02
Die Shop

模具課

Die Shop

設有模具開發部與模具維護管理部，具備全數位化模具設計、專業製造加工與檢驗能力，能快速提供開模、打樣與客製化服務。

The die shop handles mold development, maintenance, computerized design, professional fabrication, and inspection, enabling fast mold-making, samples, and customized service.

  • 模具規格包含 180、220、240、280、330、400。Mold sizes include 180, 220, 240, 280, 330, and 400.
  • 模具採 ASSAB 8407-2M 熱作工具鋼製作，管材與棒材模具鑲入碳化鎢仁以提升尺寸精度。Tooling uses ASSAB 8407-2M hot working steel; pipe and rod tooling inserts tungsten carbide to improve dimensional accuracy.

主要設備

Facilities

  • CNC 線切割加工機、CNC 綜合加工機、放電加工機、三次元精密量測機。
  • CNC wire cutting machine, CNC machining centers, EDM machines, and coordinate measuring machine.
  • 平面研磨機、帶鋸機、車床、銑床、模具專用噴砂機與 AFM 沖模機。
  • Flat lapping machine, band saw, lathe, milling machines, die sand blasting machine, and AFM machine.
03
Extrusion

擠型課

Extrusion Division

專精高品質、高精密鋁擠型材生產，結合頂尖擠型製造能力、先進數位化設備與嚴格高效的流程管理，持續精進製程與品質。

The extrusion division specializes in high-quality, high-precision aluminum profiles with advanced digital equipment, professional manufacturing capability, and strict process management.

  • 累積 50 年擠型生產經驗，監控各項製程條件，確保產品品質一致性。With 50 years of extrusion experience, each process condition is monitored to ensure consistent product quality.
  • 依顧客需求開發製程條件，包含擠型機、擠製配套設備與矯直等後段製程。Process conditions are developed around customer requirements, including extrusion presses, post-extrusion equipment, and straightening.
04
High Alloy Extrusion

高合金課

High Alloy Extrusion

因應航太、醫療、交通及軍事等產業升級需求，維新設立高強度合金生產部門，以 1100 噸及 1800 噸間接擠型自動化生產線，擠製 2000、5000、7000 系高品質高精密鋁合金材料。

To meet aerospace, medical, transportation, and defense needs, WEISHIN established high-strength alloy production with 1100-ton and 1800-ton indirect extrusion lines for 2000, 5000, and 7000 series aluminum alloys.

  • 先端設備投資與嚴格製程優化，確保材料清淨度與品質穩定性。Advanced equipment investment and strict process optimization help ensure material cleanliness and stable quality.
  • 提供穩定且高品質的高強度鋁擠型產品。Provides stable, high-quality, high-strength aluminum extrusion products.

主要設備

Facilities

  • 1800 噸間接擠型機組、1100 噸間接擠型機組。
  • 1800-ton indirect extrusion unit and 1100-ton indirect extrusion unit.
  • 退火爐、矯直機、T4 爐。
  • O-temper furnace, straightening machines, and T4 heat treatment furnace.
05
Quality Control

品保部

Quality Control

品保部負責進料、擠型製程及成品三階段品質管制，改善原物料與製程條件，並針對品質異常進行原因分析、追蹤與改善。

The quality control department manages incoming material, extrusion process, and finished profile inspection, while improving material and manufacturing conditions and tracking quality anomalies.

  • 每批鋁合金擠錠進料實施成分分析，出貨可提供材質成分分析證明。Each batch of alloy billets can be analyzed for composition, with certificates available for customers.
  • 材料機械性質、硬度、尺寸與金相組織皆可依需求檢測。Mechanical properties, hardness, dimensions, and metallurgical structures can be inspected as required.

Rapid alloy screening and mass billet sampling.

Analyzes grain structure, impurities, and material anomalies.
